The Hobbit and
The Lord of the Rings network

After Scraping the names of the characters on the Lord of the Rings Wiki website. I considered trying to detect relationships between The Hobbit and The Lord of the Rings characters from the books. And also did some graph analyses (centrality measures and community detection) and visualization using NetworkX and Pyvis. The analyses are still quite preliminary and I might have glossed over some details.
